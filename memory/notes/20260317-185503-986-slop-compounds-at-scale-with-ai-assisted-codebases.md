Counterintuitively, more codebase context does not reduce AI slop — it increases it. The model learns vocabulary, architecture names, and conventions, then produces fluent output that fits the aesthetic without fitting the logic. Fluent slop is harder to catch than generic slop. Observed by Rick at ~60k LOC.
