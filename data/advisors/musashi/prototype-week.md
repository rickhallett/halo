# Prototype Training Week

Status: TESTING (Kai is running through this to feel it out)
Source: Apple Notes, extracted 2026-04-04
Notes: Weight vest on everything may be too much for re-entry phase. Kai has a long training history but ~2 months of atrophy and fat gain. Adjust vest usage based on feel.

---

## Day A // Pull
*Vest on. Koan underneath.*

### Open (5 min)
- Sun salutation A x5, vested, controlled pace

### Main (25 min)
- **Dead hangs** 3x45s
- *Lizard pose 45s each side*
- **Pull-ups** 4x submaximal (1 from failure)
- *Deep lunge twist*
- **Chest-to-bar** or slow eccentric 3x5 (4s down)
- *Lizard pose*
- **Band pull-aparts** 4x15 (heaviest band)
- **Gravity boot rows** or shrugs 3x8

### Close (5-10 min)
- Gravity boots, 3 min
- Wheel pose, attempts not prep
- Seated forward fold, 10 breaths

`trackctl add movement --duration 30 --notes "A: pull"`

## Design Notes

- 5 training days, each ~35 min (5 open + 25 main + 5-10 close)
- Every session opens with vested sun salutations and closes with gravity boots + wheel + forward fold
- Yoga/mobility woven between strength sets, not bolted on
- 24kg kettlebell is the standard load
- Pull/Push/Legs split + skill day + KB complex day
- "Koan underneath" — contemplative practice runs concurrent with physical work
- Each session has a trackctl logging command built in
